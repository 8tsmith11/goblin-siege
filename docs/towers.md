# Towers

Towers are placed on grass tiles (not path tiles). Click a placed tower to upgrade, sell, or unlock its skill.

## Tower Stats

| Tower | Icon | Cost | DMG | Range | CD | Special | Targets |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Thoughtful Squirrel | 🐿️ | 40 | 8 | 3.2 | 50 | — | Weakest |
| Rash Lion | 🦁 | 60 | 15 | 2.0 | 25 | — | First |
| Ambitious Penguin | 🐧 | 55 | 5 | 2.8 | 35 | Slow 40% | First |
| Arrogant Fish | 🐟 | 75 | 12 | 2.5 | 60 | Splash 1.2 | First |
| Insightful Seahorse | 🦑 | 65 | 6 | 3.5 | 40 | Pierce 3 | Strongest |
| Abhorrent Lizard | 🦎 | 85 | 45 | 2.5 | 65 | Splash 1.0 · Speeds up enemies | First |
| Clever Heron | 🦩 | 70 | 10 | 3.0 | 45 | Chain 3 | Last |

**CD** = cooldown ticks between shots (lower = faster).
**Range** = radius in tiles.
**Splash** = area-of-effect radius in tiles (deals 50% damage to nearby enemies).
**Pierce** = number of additional enemies a shot passes through.
**Chain** = number of additional enemies hit by arcing lightning (at 60% damage).

The **Lizard** speeds up all enemies in range — use with caution.

## Factory 🏭

Not a combat tower — generates passive gold income.

- **Cost**: 50 gold + 25 per additional factory placed
- **Base income**: 10 + (level × 8) gold per wave
- **Upgrade (+Inc)**: 30 + level × 20 gold — increases level
- **Add Laser**: 60 gold — unlocks a red laser beam that fires at enemies in range
- **Upgrade Laser**: 30 + laserLevel × 15 gold — increases laser damage and range

The **Monkey** support boosts factory income. The **Mega Factory** and **Gold Rush** skills multiply it.
