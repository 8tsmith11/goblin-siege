## v1.5 — Considerate Fog & QoL Pass

### Considerate Fog 🌫️ (Wave 15)
- Wave 15 replaces the normal boss with a special fog event — the wave itself is the boss
- All tower range is capped to 1 tile for the entire wave
- Fog visually rolls in at wave start: 32 drifting wisps build density over 6 seconds
- Enemies that leak deal **3 lives** (same as a boss leak)
- Wave clear drops a **guaranteed artifact** at your castle
- No boss entity — just 28 mixed enemies at 1.2× HP

### Tower Augments
- Augments can now be added and removed directly from the tower tooltip
- Empty augment slot shows **+ Augment** button — clicking opens inventory to select one
- Filled slot shows the augment name with an **✕** — clicking removes it and returns it to inventory

### Feed Log Cleanup
- Weather changes and random events no longer appear in the feed
- Scribe journal updates now show a generic "The scribe has written in the journal." message
- **Elder Elderberry 🌳** speech now appears in the feed with a tree icon

### Inventory Progressive Unlock
- Inventory button is hidden until you own at least one item
- Inventory sections (Artifacts, Augments, Blueprints, Consumables) are hidden until you first own an item of that type
- Once a section unlocks it stays visible, even if you later use all items in it

### Pip's Shop
- Pip's portrait now displays correctly in the shop panel
- Removed the redundant live resource/gold ticker (resources are visible in the HUD)

### Visuals
- Rain is faster and shorter — looks like rain now instead of snow
