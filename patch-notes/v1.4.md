## v1.4 — Weather & Balance Pass

### Weather System
- Dynamic weather rolls between waves — wave 1 is always clear
- **Clear Skies** ☀️ — no effects
- **Heavy Rain** 🌧️ — monkeys move 20% slower; ground items may wash away over time
- Weather lasts a random number of waves before re-rolling; the same type can repeat
- Weather changes are logged in the feed (only when the type actually changes)

### Elder Elderberry
- Elder Elderberry 🌳 now appears on the right forest border of every map
- Speaks to you before your first wave and again at the start of wave 1 prep
- Will not spawn behind the castle exit or next to water

### Skills Overhaul (A/B/C/D)
- All combat towers now have a **D** skill — mutually exclusive with C, requires A or B
- Squirrel D: Arcane Overload — +130% DMG, -15% range
- Lion C reworked: Iron Mane — +60% DMG, hits slow enemies (replaces life-steal)
- Lion D: Relentless — +35% attack speed
- Penguin D: Ice Lance — pierce 3 enemies, +20% DMG
- Fish D: Toxic Tide — hits apply poison
- Seahorse D: Void Trap — shots stun enemies 20 ticks
- Lizard D: Scorched Earth — +50% splash radius, -25% DMG
- Heron D: Overcharge — +70% DMG, -20% range
- **Skills now apply to newly placed towers of the same type**

### Balance
- Clown reworked: single-target (targets furthest enemy on path), cooldown reduced 200→150
- Monkey Hut range reduced: 4→3 tiles
- Hoard no longer accepts augments or non-resource items from monkeys
- Monkeys no longer destroy items when delivering to a full destination — they wait and retry
- Wildfire event now correctly disables towers for the *next* wave (was broken)
- Wildfire only fires when there are more than 2 towers

### Events
- Removed: Reinforcements, Power Surge, Hoard Bonus, Bee Frenzy, Darkness
- Tax Collector: now takes 10% of current gold (was flat -30)
- Added: Scattered Stones — 5 stone items appear on random ground tiles
