## v1.6 — Slower Siege

### Pacing & Economy
- Starting gold reduced from 100g to 80g
- Most enemy types now appear at later waves — the early game is quieter
- Wave enemy counts reduced, especially in the first 10 waves
- Some enemy rewards reduced (tank, berserker, shaman, healer, shield)
- Stone sells for 1g at Pip's shop; Wood sells for 3g

### Tower Skills
- Skills are now gated behind the **Combat Training** research node
- Unlock Combat Training after researching Settlement Basics

### New Research
- **Combat Training** — unlocks the tower skill upgrade system (Settlement Basics prerequisite)

### New Content (v1.5.x backfill)
- **Proud Herald** 📯 — wave 5 boss; drops Herald's Horn artifact and a Relocation Charm
- **Augment panel** — add/remove augments directly from the tower tooltip
- **Inventory progressive unlock** — sections and button hidden until you own an item
- **Bestiary & Scribe's Journal** — new lore entries, wave-gated log entries
- **Pip's Shop** — consumable stock, blueprints, and artifact listings
- **Lab** now unlocks at Wave 5 instead of via research
- **Scribe's Journal wave-gated entries** up to wave 25
