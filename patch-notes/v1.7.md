## v1.7 — Research Gates & Elder Speaks

### Elder Elderberry
- Elder Elderberry now speaks his lines aloud using text-to-speech
- Voice is calm, low, and measured — prefers a UK English voice when available
- Three opening messages on wave 1 prep: introduction, path advice, and a hint about clicking rocks

### Research Gates
- **Workbench** is now unlocked via the **Craftsmanship** research node (prereq: Settlement Basics, cost: 8 wood + 4 stone, 1 wave)
- **Tower Skills** are now locked until you research **Combat Training** (prereq: Settlement Basics, cost: 15 dust + 5 stone, 2 waves)
- The skill panel shows a clear locked message until Combat Training is complete
