## v1.2 — Crafting & Augments

### Crafting System
- New Workbench building (⚒️) — place it to queue crafts that complete over waves
- Crafting panel shows active queue with progress and a cancel button
- Workbench accepts resource drops directly into its inventory

### Augments
- Three augments craftable at the Workbench and applicable to towers:
  - **Sharpened Flint** (🗿) — +15% damage (2 stone, 1 wave)
  - **Polished Stone** (⚪) — +10% range (2 stone, 1 wave)
  - **Taut Sinew** (🧵) — −10% attack cooldown (2 wood, 1 wave)
- Towers gain augment slots at Level 3 (1 slot) and Level 5 (2 slots)
- Apply augments from the inventory panel — select the augment, then click a tower

### Consumables
- Two placeable traps craftable at the Workbench:
  - **Stone Trap** (🪤) — deals 30 damage to the first enemy to trigger it (3 stone, 1 wave)
  - **Sticky Sap** (🍯) — slows enemies 40% for 10 seconds on contact (2 wood + 1 stone, 1 wave)
- Place on path tiles from the inventory panel — invalid tiles highlighted red

### Inventory Panel
- Augments and consumables shown in dedicated sections with action buttons
- Items automatically added when picked up from ground loot

### Balance
- Enemy gold rewards reduced across the board (normal: 4→1, tank: 8→3, shield: 9→4, boss capped at 40)
- Resource drop rates halved
- Starting gold reduced: 200→100

### Bug Fixes
- Booster monkey no longer applies duplicate boosts to the same target
- Monkey tooltip layout improved
- Monkey selection highlight now uses a bolder stroke (4px)
