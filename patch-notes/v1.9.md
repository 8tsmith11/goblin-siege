## v1.9 — Research Expansion, Lab Notes & Tower Overhaul

### New Research Nodes

- **Resonating Gem** — unlocks after Frequency Research completes. Costs 100 stone / 5 waves. Places a gem-on-pedestal structure on any empty tile. Its purpose is unclear.
- **Sweet Harvest** — prereq: Apiculture. Beehives produce 1 Honey per wave. Honey slows enemies 40% when placed on path.
- **Knowledge of the Otherworldly** — unlocks after Frequency Research + Patient Watcher appears. Allows monkeys to carry Dust to Workbenches and Stockpiles.

### Lab Notes

- Left-side feed panel removed
- Lab tower now has a **Lab Notes** tab showing the full retroactive game log
- Sort by: All · 🔮 Obs · 🌳 NPC · 👑 Boss · 🔬 Research · 📓 Scribe · 🌤️ Weather
- New **Observations** log type (🔮) — attributed to an unknown observer. Fires at key game moments:
  - Wave 1: initial defense status
  - Wave 5: the path continues past the castle
  - Wave 22: the 40Hz audio anomaly
  - First spider sighting: entity classification
  - Forge completion
  - Patient Watcher escape

### Tower Changes

**Heron** (reworked — chain lightning, not sniper)
- Base DMG: 10 → 16
- Description updated to reflect chain-lightning behavior
- New skills: Storm Arc (5 chain jumps), Focused Bolt (4× DMG, no chain), Static Field (chain slows 50% for 0.5s, non-boss), Overcharge (+80% DMG + spark bursts)
- Lightning beam rendering: jagged multi-segment bolt with glow layers and endpoint sparks; 5px thick

**Squirrel**
- Base DMG: 10 → 12 · Base rate: 44 → 40
- Upgrade costs reduced ~20%

**Beehive** — new skills
- A: Swarm Tactics (+1 bee)
- B: Venom Sting (poison 3 dmg/tick, 90 ticks)
- C: Coordinated Strike (all bees target the same enemy)
- D: Wide Range (+1.5 bee range)
- E: Mastery (+25% bee stats)

**Grateful Spider**
- Now costs 60g + 3 wood (was free)
- New D skill: **Brood Guard** — 3 orbital spiderlings circle the tower, latch onto nearby enemies, deal 2 dmg every 30 ticks until enemy escapes

**Workbench** — now costs 40g + 5 wood

### Boss Changes

- **Patient Watcher** moved to wave 50; icon changed to 👁️
- **Curious Auditor** moved to wave 30
- Vanguards fill waves 11, 17, 25, 36
- Auditor no longer speaks until first hit
- Patient Watcher hum (40Hz) now plays on escape (not only at 20% HP transition)

### Artifacts

- **Goblin Lullaby** (common, 25g) — enemies in the Lab's observation radius move 10% slower
- **Chipped Mirror** (uncommon, 40g) — one tower adjacent to the Lab fires 10% faster
- **Auditor's Ledger** — now reveals exact wave composition (enemy icons + counts) to the right of the Start button during prep

### Elder Elderberry

- Translation lines 9–12 added, gated by Pattern Recognition research and translationStep:
  - "We walk because she waits."
  - "...not enemies..."
  - "...do not look at the towers..."
  - "They are afraid of us."

### UI

- **Wave makeup display** — shows exact enemy composition (emoji x count) during prep when Auditor's Ledger is equipped, positioned to the right of the Start button
- Research completion now plays an ascending arpeggio and shows a full-screen banner
- Spider web tiles use a drawn radial web sprite (not emoji)
