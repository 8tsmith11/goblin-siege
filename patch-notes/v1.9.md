## v1.9 — Research Expansion, Lab Notes & Tower Overhaul

### New Research Nodes

- **Frequency Analysis** — hidden node, appears after the 40Hz hum plays. Costs 20 dust / 2 waves. Unlocks the Resonating Gem consumable. *"We designed a crystal that intends to capture this frequency."*
- **Knowledge of the Otherworldly** — hidden node, appears after Frequency Analysis completes AND the Patient Watcher has appeared. Costs 40 dust / 3 waves. Allows monkeys to carry Dust to Workbenches and Stockpiles.
- **Sweet Harvest** — prereq: Apiculture. Beehives produce 1 Honey per wave. Honey slows enemies 40% when placed on path.

### Resonating Gem

- Unlocked directly by completing Frequency Analysis (no separate research node)
- Placeable structure on any empty tile — no tile background, drawn gem on pedestal
- Purpose unclear

### Lab Notes

- Left-side feed panel removed
- Lab tower now has a **Lab Notes** tab — full-screen translucent overlay, pauses game
- Sort by: All · 🔮 Obs · 👤 NPC · 👺 Translations · 👑 Boss · 🔬 Research · 🎉 Events · 🌤️ Weather
- Scroll starts at newest entry (bottom)
- Goblin Translations moved from Research Web tab to Lab Notes Translations filter
- New **Observations** log type (🔮) — fires at key game moments

### Research Web

- Directional arrows on edges pointing toward unlocks
- Dev "Unlock All" now forces hidden research nodes to appear

### Tower Changes

**Heron** (buffed + reworked chain)
- Base DMG: 16 → 20 · Base range: 3.0 → 3.2 · Base rate: 45 → 42
- Chain now spreads along the path — alternates backward and forward from primary hit (no longer proximity-based)
- Mastery renamed **Tempest**: +30% DMG, chain efficiency 1.0 (full damage on all chain targets), black-and-blue jagged lightning beams

**Beehive** — new skills + Supercolony mastery
- A: Swarm Tactics (+1 bee)
- B: Venom Sting (poison 3 dmg/tick, 90 ticks)
- C: Coordinated Strike (all bees target same enemy)
- D: Wide Range (+1.5 bee range)
- E: **Mastery: Supercolony** — +2 bees, +10% bee DMG, golden pollen particle trails; on kill: 1s frenzy burst (1.5× speed)

**Grateful Spider**
- Now costs 60g + 3 wood
- D skill: **Brood Guard** — 3 orbital spiderlings circle the tower, latch onto nearby enemies

**Workbench** — now costs 40g + 5 wood

### Boss Changes

- **Patient Watcher** moved to wave 50; icon changed to 👁️
- **Curious Auditor** moved to wave 30
- Vanguards fill waves 11, 17, 25, 36
- Auditor no longer speaks until first hit
- Boss strip warning (herald horn artifact) repositioned to top-center bounded box

### Artifacts

- **Goblin Lullaby** (common, 25g) — enemies in the Lab's observation radius move 10% slower
- **Chipped Mirror** (uncommon, 40g) — one tower adjacent to the Lab fires 10% faster
- **Auditor's Ledger** — reveals exact wave composition (enemy icons + counts) to the right of the Start button during prep

### Elder Elderberry

- Fires `"...hm. The forest used to come right up to the wall."` after 10 trees are cleared
- Translation lines 9–12 now appear in Lab Notes Translations filter

### UI

- **Wave makeup display** — shows exact enemy composition during prep when Auditor's Ledger is equipped
- Research completion plays ascending arpeggio and shows full-screen banner
- Spider web tiles use drawn radial web sprite
